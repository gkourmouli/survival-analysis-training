WEIBULL MODEL — LOG-LIKELIHOOD OVER SCALE λ AND SHAPE γ

Profile Log-Likelihood ℓ(λ | γ̂)

The Weibull has two parameters — scale λ and shape γ. The profile likelihood fixes one parameter at its MLE and traces the log-likelihood over the other. Notice the asymmetry: both parameters are positive, so the curve has a longer right tail than a symmetric parabola.

ℓ(λ,γ) = n·logγ − n·γ·logλ + (γ−1)·Σlog tᵢ − Σ(tᵢ/λ)^γ
MLE: solved numerically  |  No closed form for γ̂ , but λ̂ = (Σtᵢ^γ̂/n)^(1/γ̂)
